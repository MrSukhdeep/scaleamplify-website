Data Sovereignty & Compliance

Scale Amplify is committed to full compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). We process data based on legitimate interest for B2B outreach and ensure all data subjects have clear paths to exercise their rights.

Key Principles

  • Transparency: Clear disclosure of data collection and processing activities.
  • Accuracy: Maintaining up-to-date and precise professional data.
  • Security: Enterprise-grade protection for all handled information.
  • Accountability: Regular audits of our data processing engines.
